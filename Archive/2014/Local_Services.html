Aqua Water Quality Reports

As requested by a resident, here is a link to the 2007-2008 Aqua Water Quality Reports for Springdale. The 2008-2009 report will come out August 1, 2009, according to Aqua customer service. View the annual water quality report search page here. Input "Springdale" into the "Enter System Name" field to pull Springdale's water quality report. Contact their Customer Service Department at 1-877-987-2782 for a hard copy of the report.
