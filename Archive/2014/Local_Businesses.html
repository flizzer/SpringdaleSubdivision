Visit the Stokesdale Business Association's Website First!

The Stokesdale Business Association has taken off! You can find many businesses in Stokesdale listed on their website, along with their calendar of events and other interesting information. Please visit their website at www.stokesdalebusinessassociation.com. The businesses that are listed on the association's website have paid for membership so that you, neighbors, can benefit from information about your local businesses; how to contact them, where they are located, etc. Did you receive the directory of Stokesdale businesses in your mailbox? That directory was the product of a lot of hard work for your convenience. Listed below are some of the businesses in Stokesdale that may or may not yet be listed on the Association's site. I'd suggest visiting the association's site first, then look here.

Springdale is located at the northern end of Stokesdale, NC. Patronizing Stokesdale's businesses, and businesses of those working in Springdale, will help to improve the economy of our great city and subdivision. The information below is provided to you as a guide to these local businesses! For more information on the city of Stokesdale, check out the city's website at www.stokesdale.org.

This list may not be complete.

This information is meant for your convenience. None of the businesses listed below sponsor this website. Most of the information below was taken from other advertisements. If you know of any other businesses to add to this list, please contact the Webmaster at SpringdaleWebmaster@gmail.com.

Attention Businesses!

If you are located in Stokesdale, NC, and would like to add your business to the list, to update or add to the information about your business, or to be removed from the list, please contact SpringdaleWebmaster@gmail.com.
