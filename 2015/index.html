Neighborhood Watch

We look out for each other in this neighborhood! The Neighborhood Watch sends out E-mail alerts to residents warning of potential safety issues in Springdale. If you would like to be included on this E-mail list, or if you have an alert, please send an E-mail to the Neighborhood Watch Spokesperson, Libby Kendrick, at libbyakendrick@gmail.com. If you see a potential threat to the safety of the residents in Springdale, please inform the local police via the non-emergency line, 336-373-2222.

Looking for leaders for the neighborhood watch! Libby Kendrick is our Spokesperson, however, the Neighborhood Watch is in need of some volunteers to organize the group meetings. According to Corporal Fuller, we should be having monthly, quarterly, or yearly meetings.
